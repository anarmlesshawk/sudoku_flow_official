Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version: Sudoku Flow does not collect, store, or share your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Sudoku Flow does not collect any personally identifiable information. The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your game progress, scores, and statistics
  • Your streak and daily puzzle completion history
  • Your app settings and theme preferences
  • Any in-app purchases you have made (stored locally)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

2. Advertising

Sudoku Flow displays banner advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These ad networks may collect certain non-personal technical information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • General location (country or region, not precise location)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

Users who purchase the "Remove Ads" option will not be shown any advertisements, and ad network SDKs will not be active for those users.

For more information about how ad networks handle data, please refer to Google's advertising privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

3. In-App Purchases

Sudoku Flow offers optional one-time in-app purchases (premium themes and ad removal). Purchases are processed through Google Play's billing system. We do not receive or store your payment information. Please refer to Google Play's terms of service for details on how payment data is handled.

4. Permissions

Sudoku Flow may request the following device permissions:

  • Notifications — to send optional daily puzzle reminders. You can decline this permission or disable it at any time in your device settings.
  • Internet — required to load the app and display advertisements.

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device features.

5. Children's Privacy

Sudoku Flow is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

6. Data Retention

Since all app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data through your device settings.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may include links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
